Bi-weekly Reflection 7

Last Updated: Feb 16, 2026

This reflection marks the conclusion of all progress on this project. The next reflection will be my Final Reflection, and everything will be completed in this project.

Over the past few days, I have finished programming and stress testing the security alarm project. It was a pain at first. I had trouble with the PIR sensor again. It triggered action whenever it detected a movement, even when it was not supposed to. I didn’t have an actual “solution” to this issue. Instead, I “worked around” the problem. I made extra variables to define whether or not it should execute the action, regardless of the sensor data. It was painful, and it took me a few days to work it out. I’ve recorded the videos for the traffic lights and the security alarm demonstration. I’ve also finished up the descriptions for the projects.

The extension of winter break helped me a lot with my project. I was worried that I wouldn’t be able to finish up my third final product by the end of the winter break. In that case, I will have to bring my project to school to work on. However, the West Coast is raining most of the time, making bringing electronics to school a hard task. In addition, my board is too long to fit into my locker, which makes things even harder. With winter break extended, I used the extra time to finish up the final bits. Now, I only have the reflections and portfolio stuff to finish up.

Over the next week, I will finalize my portfolio and write the Final Reflection. Then, I should be all set for my presentation.
